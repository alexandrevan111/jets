Support Jets

Jets is an open source project and completely free to use. Developing and maintaining the project requires a significant amount of time and effort. You can support Jets development to keep it sustainable.

If your company is using Jets as a part of your product, it benefits you to to sponsor Jets development. Sponsorship ensures that the software that your product relies on stays healthy and is actively maintained. It also provides you with exposure in the Jets community and makes it easier to attract developers.

If you are an individual user and have gotten value out of using Jets, consider donating out of the goodness of your heart. It helps make Jets better.

We love our sponsors! Reach us at contact@boltops.com for more info.
